USS Scorpio, please respond..

Sci-fi Horror Adventure

In the cold void of space, a distress signal from the USS Scorpio draws your crew into a deadly encounter. Aboard the derelict vessel, alien gas has unleashed grotesque mutations, and every corridor hides new terrors. Time is running out before the ship, and everyone on it, is destroyed.

This tense sci-fi horror pamphlet adventure for Mothership® drops your players into a high-stakes mission of survival and mystery. Search for survivors, contain the mutagen, and escape before the Global Mesh Emergency Protocol seals your fate.

What's Included:

  • Two-page pamphlet adventure
  • Detailed ship map with key locations
  • Mutagenic infection and mutation rules
  • Unique NPCs with hidden agendas
  • Deadly alien threats and environmental hazards
  • Adventure hooks and GM guidance

Stellar Frontiers

Sci-Fi Exploration Adventure

Embark on an epic journey across the galaxy in Stellar Frontiers, where humanity has taken its first steps into the vast cosmos. As interstellar explorers, players will discover new worlds, encounter alien civilizations, and uncover the mysteries of deep space.

This science fiction RPG combines hard sci-fi elements with thrilling space opera adventure. Build your crew, customize your starship, and navigate the political complexities of a galaxy filled with wonder and danger.

What's Included:

  • Complete core rulebook (300+ pages)
  • Starship construction system
  • 15 alien species to encounter
  • Galaxy-spanning campaign
  • Advanced technology rules
  • Space combat mechanics

Whispers in the Fog

Mystery Victorian Investigation

Step into the fog-shrouded streets of Victorian London, where gaslight flickers against the darkness and supernatural mysteries lurk around every corner. As investigators of the paranormal, players must uncover conspiracies that threaten the very fabric of reality.

This atmospheric mystery RPG blends historical authenticity with supernatural horror. Use deduction, investigation, and courage to solve cases that the authorities dare not touch. But beware—some knowledge comes at a terrible price.

What's Included:

  • Victorian London setting guide
  • Investigation mechanics system
  • 8 interconnected mystery scenarios
  • Period-accurate equipment and rules
  • Sanity and horror mechanics
  • Historical NPCs and locations

About Yellow Goat Games

Yellow Goat Games is a new indie collaboration between Swedish RPG creators Mikael Ribeiro (USS Scorpio, Please Respond, Atlas Bearing the Heavens) and Tomas Eriksson (Wanted! Borg, Destination Hollow Ground).

Between us, we’ve built derelict starships, wandering gods, and feasts you might regret attending. Our games lean into strange worlds, sharp choices, and stories that stay with you long after the dice stop rolling.

Now, we’ve joined forces to work on our first joint release, Next Stop: Hell’s Gate, a tabletop RPG adventure of cosmic dread, desperate choices, and high stakes. It’s only the beginning, but we’re taking the scenic route through the weird and the wonderful.
